- Kim, S. (2017). A survey on postsecondary Korean language programs in the United States. *Journal of the National Council of Less Commonly Taught Languages*, *21*, 99-126. [link](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/321887612_A_Survey_on_Postsecondary_Korean_Language_Programs_in_the_United_States)

- Kim, S., & Yoon, H.-J. (2016). Stop using that: Expressing definiteness in Korean. *MSU Working Papers in Second Language Studies*, *7*, 44-56. [link](http://sls.msu.edu/soslap/journal/index.php/sls/article/download/80/63)

- Kim, S. (2018, March). Capital, Ideology, and Value Creation: A case study of an American learner of Korean. Paper presented at the American Association for Applied Linguistics (AAAL) conference, Chicago, Illinois, USA.

- Kim, S., & Park, O.-S. (2016, October). Validation of a Korean Speaking Proficiency Assessment. Poster presented at the Midwest Association of Language Testers (MwALT) conference, West Lafayette, Indiana, USA.
